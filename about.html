About Us

MathWise was founded with a vision to enhance the learning experience of high school students struggling with mathematics. Our journey began in collaboration with the LASA High School math department, aiming to provide students with an innovative AI-powered tool that offers step-by-step guidance, personalized tips, and detailed feedback on their math problems. We believe in leveraging AI technology to support education, making complex mathematical concepts more accessible and understandable.

The Creators

Hadeed Khan: Passionate about education and technology, Hadeed Khan co-founded MathWise to bridge the gap between students and effective math learning. With a background in computer science, Hadeed brings innovative AI solutions to the educational sphere.

Jeffrey Norman: With a deep interest in mathematics and its teaching methodologies, Jeffrey Norman co-founded MathWise to help students excel in math. His experience in math education ensures that the AI tool provides accurate and helpful guidance.
