About Us

At MathWise, we believe in using AI not to hurt students' education, but to assist it and help it grow. In collaboration with the LASA High School math department, our program helps you get a push when starting math problems. Our application scans the problem and any work given and gives you tips like how to start, what formula to use, and where you went wrong.

Features

  • Step-by-Step Assistance

How it works

MathWise uses artificial intelligence to read, process, and give feedback on progress and on the question as well as to advise the user where to study and review from in the given curriculum.
